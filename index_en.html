Company Founder

The founder of Huizhou Tingyukang Bag Co., Ltd., a visionary and innovative entrepreneur, is highly respected in the industry.

With keen insights into market trends and relentless pursuit of quality and design, he successfully transformed a small workshop into a globally recognized bag brand today.

The founder has always focused on customer needs, advocating teamwork and open communication, embedding these principles into the company culture. He believes that continuous innovation and improvement are essential to maintain a leading position in the competitive market, which is key to the company's ongoing growth and strength.

Company Introduction

Huizhou Tingyukang Bag Co., Ltd., located in Huizhou City, Guangdong Province, China, specializes in the design, production, and sales of bags. Founded in the early 21st century, the company quickly established a foothold in the domestic and international bag markets with its extensive industry experience and sharp market insights.

The company is known for producing a wide range of fashionable and durable bag products, including backpacks, travel bags, computer bags, and handbags, catering to various consumer needs.

Huizhou Tingyukang Bag Co., Ltd. emphasizes product quality and innovative design, blending traditional craftsmanship with modern technology. The company has a skilled and experienced design and production team that continuously introduces market-driven and innovative products.

Corporate Culture

Huizhou Tingyukang Bag Co., Ltd. has been deeply rooted in the bag industry for many years, always adhering to the business philosophy of "quality first, innovation driven." The company views its corporate culture as the soul of its progress, emphasizing teamwork, continuous learning, and customer-first values.

Scope of Business

Processing, wholesale, retail: bags, leather goods, crafts (excluding ivory and its products), plastic products, toys, metal accessories; e-commerce; import and export of goods or technology.
